### ğŸš€ A Brief History

Launched by **Binance** in **September 2020**, Binance Smart Chain (BSC) was designed as a high-performance, low-cost alternative to Ethereum. While Ethereum pioneered smart contracts, its rising gas fees created accessibility issues. BSC introduced the **BEP20 token standard** and a dual-chain architecture that allows users to move assets between Binance Chain and BSC.

From the beginning, BSC prioritized **speed**, **efficiency**, and **developer-friendliness**, making it the go-to chain for DeFi, NFTs, and high-frequency transactions. Within its first year, BSC exploded in popularity, hosting thousands of dApps and surpassing Ethereum in daily transaction count.
